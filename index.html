Never Miss a Lead Again

Hisband Voice is an AI calling agent that instantly engages your leads, books site visits, and syncs with your calendar, so you can focus on building.

How It Works

1. Upload Your Leads

Easily upload a CSV or Excel file of your leads. Our system parses and prepares them for calling in seconds.

2. AI Agent Auto-Calls

Our conversational AI agent calls each lead, qualifies them, and answers their basic questions in a natural, human-like voice.

3. Visits Booked & Synced

When a lead is ready, the AI books a site visit directly into your Google Calendar and sends a WhatsApp confirmation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What languages does the AI support?

Currently, our AI is optimized for Hinglish (a mix of Hindi and English) to best serve the Indian real estate market. More languages are coming soon.

How do I integrate my calendar?

During onboarding, you'll be prompted to connect your Google Account. Our system uses the Google Calendar API to find available slots and book appointments automatically.

What are the costs?

We offer a free trial to get you started. After that, our pricing is based on the number of calls made per month. Contact us for detailed enterprise plans.
